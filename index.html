ABOUT

My name is Ty Enger. I am a self-taught Front-End Developer currently working with Foremost Media; a website design, development, SEO, and digital marketing agency in Janesville, WI. I am experienced in HTML and CSS, and steadily developing my knowledge of Javascript and related frameworks. Professionally, I work primarily with DotNetNuke - a C# based CMS, Wordpress, Git, SCSS, and Razor Templates.

My passion for web development began about a year and a half ago when my I was recruited by my wife's grandpa to develop a website to market his Kentucky Bourbon Tours. I have been hooked ever since. I dove head first into online courses and landed a position with Foremost Media at the start of 2022. Through my personal and professional experience, I have developed my skills tremendously and look forward to the life-long learning journey that web development offers. I seek to make the web a more expressive and accessible place for users and strive to do great work that influences millions of people.
